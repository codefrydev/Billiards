How to Play 8-Ball

  • Shoot: Click or tap, then drag back to aim the cue stick. Release to strike.
  • Rules: First ball potted determines your suit (Solids or Stripes).
  • Winning: Clear your suit, then pot the 8-Ball (Black).
  • Foul: Potting the cue ball gives the opponent "Ball in Hand" (drag to place).
